# FAUNA Research Skill

Use this skill before broad FAUNA adoption or schema hardening decisions.

## Research pass

1. Compare the proposed animal-life model to at least three grounded references:
   ecology, animal husbandry, wildlife movement, disease ecology, or game-system
   precedent.
2. Separate evidence-backed animal behavior from speculative game pressure.
3. Identify which downstream repo owns interpretation or mechanics.
4. Record citations in the relevant FAUNA wave or pulse doc before adoption.
