# FAUNA Phases

## Phase 1 - FAUNA Foundation

Establish the repo, Rust primitives, seed fixture, validation command, and
dependency placement for animal-life records.

Exit criteria:

- `fauna-core` and `fauna-cli` validate the seed fauna fixture.
- PRODUCT_PLAN names species, habitat, behavior, pressure, and evidence
  boundaries.
- TRACKER records FAUNA as a Knowledge Systems submodule.
- Dependency trackers identify planned PROOF/CROP/PEBBLE/FLETCH use and deferred
  RLINE extraction.

## Later phases

- Consumer probes for BANISH, PORTO, CERES, LUCIA, and CANON.
- Publisher artifacts for CROP, PEBBLE, FLETCH, and PROOF.
- Extraction decision for any repeated ecology or animal-behavior primitives.
