# FAUNA Review Organization

FAUNA reviews protect living animal pressure without turning FAUNA into a game
mechanics, economy, geography, or full ecology simulator.

## Panel

| ID | Role | Lens |
|---|---|---|
| F-1 | Species Steward | Species records, roles, behavior, and habitat fit |
| F-2 | Behavior Ecologist | Movement, reproduction, predation, pest pressure, and vectors |
| F-3 | Consumer Adapter | BANISH, CERES, PORTO, LUCIA, CANON, and TRACKER fixture usefulness |
| F-4 | Evidence Custodian | Source refs, anchors, and pack readiness |
| F-5 | Boundary Skeptic | Keeps maps, economics, ritual, interpretation, and mechanics out |

## Review order

1. Use the panel for species/habitat schema changes and consumer fixtures.
2. Use the Boundary Skeptic before accepting broad ecology simulation behavior.
3. Use the Evidence Custodian before publishing animal-life packs.
